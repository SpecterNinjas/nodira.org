About Company



Section 1.1: Startup Overview

nodira.org is a startup web development site containing Python tutorial with quick references and most demanded topics.

This web development site has been founded because of frustration to the current web development site since most of fail to provide good programming resources and guidance step by step.

In current market, there are lots of sites that can provide good quality content and articles based on your interests, but the problem is they are not free.

Currently, we decided to start our journey with Python Programming since it is the most loved and demanded programming language that can pave a way for your dream job in giant internet companies.

Section 1.2: Our Mission

At nodira, we believe that anyone can learn anything. All it takes is a tenacious spirit, the right guidance, and the tools to see it through. We know that students are under more pressure than ever. Hence, it’s our job to give every student the tools and confidence to succeed, no matter what their motivation, or what they’re striving to achieve.

We believe that learning should be free and we are here to make it possible. At nodira, you can learn web development for free forever and it's our main goal to provide good quality content and step by step guidance for each and every user for free!

Section 1.3: Why should I learn at nodira.org ?
